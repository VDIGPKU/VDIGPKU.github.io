About Us

The VDIG Lab is committed to research in perception directions such as computer vision and deep learning. In the past five years, we have published more than 30 papers in important journals or conferences such as IJCV, TIP, PR, NeurIPS, ICCV, CVPR, ECCV, AAAI, and MM. We have applied for more than 20 Chinese invention patents, and obtained 7 authorizations. Several technical achievements have been put into practical use in corresponding products or prototypes of Alibaba, Ant Financial, Baidu, Hisense, and China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation. We have achieved good results in top competitions for autonomous vehicle/drone scene perception (WAD of CVPR 2018, VisDrone of ECCV 2018, MS COCO, nuScenes Detection, etc.). The laboratory has undertaken more than ten scientific research projects with funding of more than 16 million yuan, including 3 National Natural Science Foundation projects, 1 Scientific and Technological Innovation 2030-Major project topic, 1 173 Plan Key project topic, National Key R&D Plan project topic, Beijing Natural Science Foundation, and horizontal cooperation projects with well-known companies such as Changan, Alibaba, and Ant Financial.
